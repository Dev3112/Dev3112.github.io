About Us

25 years of experience in the industry comes alive in Aarvi, the epitome of rock solid testing and innovative switches.

  • Established in year 2017, Aarvi Electrical Industries is a leading Manufacturer of an exclusive range of Modular Switches and Coverplates, Bulb Holder, Fan Plates and more.
  • The offered products are manufactured using supreme quality raw material and latest technology under the strict supervision of our skilled professionals. Under the visionary guidance of Rajen Ramanuj(Partner) and Vijay Khambhadiya (Partner), our organization is succeeding towards higher strides of growth in the industry.

Strong, secure and safe, Aarvi's products are innovative and provide maximum value to its users. Every product is thoroughly tested and certified, replete with electrical and mechanical strength, they are resistant to heat, fire, humidity and aging. Our impeccable products speak for us as proudly as we vouch for them.
